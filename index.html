Growing up on a farm taught me the value of hard work and problem-solving from an early age. These formative years instilled in me a strong work ethic and the ability to tackle challenges head-on, qualities that have served me well in both my professional and academic pursuits.

                        
At 13, my uncle introduced me to coding, sparking a passion that would shape my future. I quickly mastered HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, building my first websites within months. This early exposure to technology ignited my curiosity and set me on the path to becoming a developer.

                        
Now pursuing Computer Science in college, I bring the same determination I developed doing farm work and fixing equipment to my software development. My unique background gives me a practical perspective on problem-solving and a relentless drive to create efficient, effective solutions.

                        
When I'm not coding, you can find me working on personal projects, learning new technologies, or enjoying outdoor activities. I believe in continuous learning and pushing beyond my comfort zone to grow both personally and professionally.
